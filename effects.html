Effects of Flaming (Cyberbullying)

Raising awareness about online harassment.

Statistics

  • Over 50% of young people have experienced flaming online.
  • Flaming increases risk of anxiety and depression by 30%.
  • Those who are flamed are 2-3 times more likely to consider suicide.

Psychological Effects

  • Low self-esteem
  • Withdrawal from friends and activities
  • Loss of interest in school and hobbies
  • Insomnia, anxiety, depression
