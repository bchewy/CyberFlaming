How to Stop Flaming

Stopping flaming in cyberbullying requires a combination of preventive measures, intervention strategies, and fostering a positive online environment.

Responding to Cyberbullying

Everyone plays a part in making our environment free of flaming. An immediate response is necessary to stop and manage flaming.

Youths: If you are being flaming, do something about it.

Parents: Parents and other carers have a vital role in responding to bullying.

Schools: Building a school culture of zero tolerance for bullying involves various initiatives.

Dealing with Cyberbullying

What you should do when faced with flaming:

  • Stop responding to the cyberbully and log off the communication device.
    Cyberbullies often want to get a reaction from their victims. By not responding or retaliating, the bullies are unable to achieve their objective and may stop their flaming.
